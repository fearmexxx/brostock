# BroStock Progress Log - March 16, 2026

## Summary
Upgraded the core analysis engine to the **Institutional Multi-Factor Trading Signal Framework**, introducing a sophisticated -100 to +100 Conviction Score.

## Key Changes

### 1. Signal Engine Upgrade (`stock_analyzer.py`)
- **New Architecture:** Implemented a five-factor model:
    - Trend (30%): SMA cross & Price position.
    - Momentum (20%): RSI, MACD, ROC.
    - Volume Flow (15%): Volume Surge, OBV, VWAP.
    - Volatility (15%): ATR & Bollinger Band width.
    - Mean Reversion (20%): RSI & BB extremes.
- **Dynamic Weighting:** Integrated ADX-based regime detection. Weights shift between Trend-following and Mean-reversion based on market strength.
- **Conviction Score:** Transitioned from -10/+10 to -100/+100 scale for higher precision.

### 2. Backend & Bot Enhancements (`backend/main.py`)
- **Telegram Bot v2.5:**
    - Updated `/price` to show factor breakdowns (Trend, Momentum, etc.).
    - Added Market Regime detection (Trending/Range) with ADX.
    - Localized labels for the new conviction levels (e.g., "MUA MẠNH", "TIÊU CỰC").
- **Market Scanning:** Adjusted global scan thresholds to align with the new scoring range.

### 3. Frontend Dashboard (`frontend/components/DashboardClient.tsx`)
- **Conviction Sidebar:** Replaced simple Bull/Bear indicators with a detailed factor breakdown UI.
- **Regime Indicator:** Added a visual ADX-based market regime meter.
- **Refined UI:** Improved readability of signal scores and 5-day outlooks.

### 4. Backtester Synchronization (`backtester.py`)
- **Vectorized Logic:** Ported the Institutional Factor logic to the backtester using optimized Pandas operations.
- **Threshold Updates:** Updated buy/sell signals to use +/- 40 conviction score thresholds.
